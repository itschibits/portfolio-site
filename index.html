About Me

I like to do so many things that I never know how to introduce myself! I love building on ideas, which has led me to consult for startups, learning how to build a video game, and embark on a months long journey to try starting my own company. I'm in my best element when I'm uncomfortable. This means working on the unknown, challenging myself to get over fears, and finding the next point of improvement.

I started my career in software engineering at Rithm School, where I learned mostly web development using Python and JavaScript. After a short internship at Foqal, a customer support integration for Slack, I am currently an Associate Software Engineer at athenahealth!

Outside of work, I am an enthusiast Twitch streamer, aspiring vocalist, and viewer of way too many cat videos. I love travelling, all sorts of board games and video games (minus the scary ones), going out for karaoke, and trying out new restaurants.
